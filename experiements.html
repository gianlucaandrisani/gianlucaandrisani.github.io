Experiments

Through the skills learnt from the VR mobile apps online course emitted from San Diego University that I pursued in November, I applied my knowledge in some experiments with AR and VR mobile applications. I decided to go through this path in order to understand how users interact with immersive digital systems and to find the right solutions for them in order to refine and make more spontaneous the interaction between human and digital worlds.
